Locations

Based in Bexhill, we operate our events all over East Sussex, and cover other areas too for an additional cost.

We do provide a fixed location where we can host your event, if you wish to however provide your own location, the location must meet some requirements.

Location Requirements:

  • The location must have available parking
  • The location must be close to a power outlet (20m)
  • The location must have sufficient space for an extra large wheeled, high roof van
  • The location must have sufficient space for clients to get in the back of the van safely
  • The location must not be on a slope
  • The location must not block footpaths
  • The location must not be on a junction
  • The location must not pose a risk of penalty charge
  • The location must not pose a risk to members of the public (extension leads etc.)

If the location you provide is a paid parking area, you must cover these fees.

Don't hesitate to contact us if you have trouble finding a suitable location, we can assist you in this, or you may use our fixed location.
