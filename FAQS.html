FAQS

How much is the event?

Our parties start from £150 for 1 hour, or £190 for 90 minutes of gametime, or £240 for 2 hours of gametime!

How do I book?

You can book your event by contacting us, find contact details here.

Does an adult need to be present?

For childrens parties, a responsible adult must be present on arrival, we can then supervise the children once the party gets going. All of our staff are DBS checked.

Which areas do you operate in?

We cover East Sussex, and for an additional cost the surrounding areas also.

How far in advance do I need to book?

Ideally as soon as possible, but we advise at least 30 days before your intended date.

Are there any age restrictions?

There are no age restrictions regarding who can attend our events. Minors however must be accompanied by a responsible adult upon arrival. Available games will be age restricted to the youngest member of the group, according to PEGI ratings.

Can we bring our own food?

Unfortunately food or drink cannot be consumed inside the van.

How long are the gaming sessions?

We offer flexible bookings to suit your needs.

Will the weather affect our booking?

Only in extreme cases. In such circumstances, we can arrange a new date, amendment, or refund.

Are you beginner friendly?

We have friendly staff on hand who are passionate about gaming and will be happy to ensure that everyone enjoys their time with us.

How close must the power outlet be?

We provide an extension cable of 20 meters, so it must be within that range.

Do I need to provide an internet connection?

Our van has 2 5G routers to enable online gaming. If however there is poor signal in the area of your booking, it would be better suited if we could connect to your router.
